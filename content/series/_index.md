## What Are Series?

Series are collections of related blog posts that work together to explore a topic comprehensively. Each series:

- **Builds progressively**: Start with fundamentals and advance to complex topics
- **Interconnected**: Posts reference and build upon each other
- **Comprehensive**: Cover topics more thoroughly than standalone articles
- **Ordered**: Read in sequence for the best learning experience
